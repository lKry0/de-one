DE ONE Construction

About Us

DE ONE Construction is a Los Angeles–based General Contractor led by Denys Tarasov, bringing over a decade of hands-on experience in residential and commercial construction across Ukraine, Europe, and California.

Experience & Expertise

Denys has managed new construction and renovation projects from early planning through final delivery, overseeing site preparation, structural work, trade coordination, and finish construction. His background combines European construction discipline and craftsmanship with California building codes, safety standards, and project management practices, ensuring precise execution and reliable results.

Our Approach

Today, DE ONE Construction delivers projects with a structured and transparent approach — whether for individual construction trades or fully managed, end-to-end builds. Each project is guided by clear accountability, consistent quality, and attention to detail.

Community Commitment

In addition to his work in construction, Denys is the Founder and President of the nonprofit organization Protection of War Victims, reflecting a strong commitment to integrity, responsibility, and community impact — values that shape the way DE ONE Construction operates.
